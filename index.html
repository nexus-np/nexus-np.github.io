About Us

We are Nexus, a passionate team of high school students driven by a common goal, to make a difference in our community through STEM. Through technology, outreach, and collaboration, we connect bright ideas with the resources they need to become reality. Our mission is to build a nexus of creativity, compassion, and change, all powered by the energy and vision of students like us.
