This Year's Theme: Edutech

The field of education is ripe for disruption. From personalized learning paths powered by AI to immersive AR/VR classrooms, the possibilities are endless.

We're challenging you to build a project that addresses a key problem in education. This could be a tool for:

  • Student accessibility and inclusion
  • Teacher automation and support
  • Gamified learning and engagement
  • Lifelong learning and skill development
